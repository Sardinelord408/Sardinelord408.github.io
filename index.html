Recent Projects

The Last Star

The Last Star is a First-Person Shooter, Sci-Fi Survival-Horror game developed in Unreal Engine. On board of a spaceship 300 trillion years in the future. The player's goal is to save humanity from the perpetual darkness of the end of the universe by surviving murderous automatons and activate a time portal that will allow humanity to survive its extinction.

ITER

ITER is a 3rd person adventure and exploration game where the player is a child who wakes up on an island that was once a great civilization whose energy came from music, now lifeless. The player must restore life to the island with a mysterious guitar through the power of music and find his way back home through various challenges and uncover the mysteries that the island hides. This project was submited for the Playstation Talents 2018 in Portugal.

Get off my island

Winner of the GAME JAM + PORTO 2019. This videogame is a 3D Top-Down Shooter in which a retired man on his past adventures retrieved an emerald from a lost kingdom, and as a result he now has to defend his home on a secluded island from creatures of the deep that have come to claim it back.

Feudal Repair Bot

A videogame developed for the Global Game Jam 2020. A 3D Top-Down point and click set in a medieval post-apocalyptic world, which is inhabited by sentient robots. The game focuses around the goal of the game jams' theme of "repair" since the player needs to repair this broken world in order to achieve a prosperous future for all.

White Dwarf

A videogame developed in Unreal Engine for the Global Game Jam 2019. The player takes control of a worker robot in a space station around the last star in the universe that gained consciousness after being deactivated for centuries. After glancing at the great deeds of humanity and realizing that these species are alive in the station his goal becomes to collect 4 keys to have access to the white dwarf energy component and save humanity by activating a time portal with its energy, or not.
